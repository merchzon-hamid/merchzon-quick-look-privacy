Merchzon Quick look – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Merchzon Quick look is a Chrome extension that displays Amazon product information such as ASIN, Date First Available, Best Sellers Rank, and review count directly on Amazon pages.

Data collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data about you or your browsing activity.

Specifically, the extension does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Browsing history across sites
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking information

How the extension works

All product information shown by the extension is read directly from the Amazon page you have open in your browser. The processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers.

Third parties

Merchzon Quick look does not sell, rent, or transfer any data to third parties.
